What We Do

This website is designed to act as a search engine for people to find volunteer organizations near them to give back to their community. Being a volunteer is a satisfactory hobby, and a fun way to be more involved with the world. You can improve your networking and social life, so why not give it a try? Find a location near you and a type of volunteer service to get started!
